Cookies

Description

This is a simple and delicious cookies recipe. You can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. Here are the ingredients you'll need:

  • Butter: You'll need unsalted butter at room temperature.
  • Sugar: Both granulated sugar and brown sugar are needed.
  • Eggs: You'll need one large egg.

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Beat in the eggs and butter.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let the cookies cool before serving.
