Pancakes

Description

This is a simple and delicious pancake recipe. You can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. Here are the ingredients you'll need:

  • Flour: You'll need all-purpose flour for this recipe.
  • Sugar: A small amount of sugar for a touch of sweetness.
  • Eggs: You'll need one large egg.
  • Milk: Any type of milk will work.
  • Baking Powder: This helps the pancakes rise.

Steps

  1. Mix together the dry ingredients.
  2. Beat in the egg and milk.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
